About our mission

Invisible Patients Mission exists to improve health outcomes for people who are routinely overlooked. We lift up the voices of patients and caregivers while equipping clinicians with the tools to deliver equitable, compassionate care.

Our story

We started by listening. Patients and caregivers told us that they felt dismissed, burdened by complex systems, and often left to manage care alone. We built this nonprofit to change that experience.

Today, we collaborate with community organizations, care teams, and advocates to build programs that meet real needs.

Our values

  • Equity and cultural humility
  • Plain language and transparency
  • Partnership with patients and caregivers
  • Respect for clinician time and expertise

Who we serve

Patients

People living with chronic illness, disability, or complex care needs.

Caregivers

Family members and community supporters navigating care alongside loved ones.

Providers

Clinicians and care teams seeking equitable, trauma-informed resources.
